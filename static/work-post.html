Universal Music

Year 2011-2012

I worked with Universal Music for over a year, helping maintain their large network of artist and label websites. After we released the new Fiction Records website I built the company’s first responsive front end for the Mercury Records label website.

The next responsive front end I built was for Sinfini Music, Universal’s new flagship classical music website. This began with a simple WordPress blog and soon after we launched the Umbraco-driven main site which is fast becoming the difinitive resource for newcomers to classical music.
